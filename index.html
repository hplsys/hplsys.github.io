We are a team of researchers specializing in parallel computing, compilers, and programming languages. We are particularly interested in exploring compiler and runtime techniques to facilitate the development of high-performance and parallel programs for data-intensive and machine-learning applications. We are part of the Computer Science Department at the University of Iowa.
